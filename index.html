Biomass Pyrolysis in a Fluidized Bed

Coupled multi-scale model combining intra-particle transport, pyrolysis kinetics, and a 2D fluidized bed reactor using a progressive conversion scheme.

CO₂-to-Methanol Process Design

Integrated Aspen Plus model for CO₂ capture from cement flue gas and conversion to methanol via catalytic hydrogenation, emphasizing process efficiency and circular carbon utilization.

Biodiesel Production in RS-SDR

Mechanistic modeling of three-phase (liquid-liquid-solid) transesterification in RS-SDR: homogeneous, slurry, and coated-disc systems.

CO₂ Capture in Cement Plants

Simulation and techno-economic analysis of MEA-based CO₂ capture for cement plants, optimizing absorber-stripper configurations and energy use.

Multiphase CFD Projects

Droplet impact, heat transfer, spray modeling, and fluidized beds in OpenFOAM — benchmarked, validated, and compared.

Catalysis Projects

Mechanistic analysis, kinetic modeling, and numerical simulation of catalytic systems, covering CO₂ methanation over inverse CeOx/Co catalysts, methanol synthesis kinetics, and reaction–diffusion behavior.

Particle-Based Simulation Projects

Molecular dynamics, DPD, and DEM simulations exploring planetary motion, supercritical mixtures, and granular flow in rotating drums.

Data Science & ML Projects

Regression, classification, and interpretability in real-world datasets: housing prices and peer-to-peer lending risks.
